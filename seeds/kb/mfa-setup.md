# How to Set Up MFA on a New Laptop

Multi-Factor Authentication (MFA) is required for all company devices. Follow these steps to set up MFA on your new laptop.

## Prerequisites
- New laptop with Windows or macOS installed
- Access to your work email
- Authenticator app (Microsoft Authenticator recommended)

## Steps

1. **Connect to Company Network**
   - Connect to the company Wi-Fi or use an ethernet cable
   - Ensure you have internet connectivity

2. **Open Microsoft Authenticator App**
   - Download Microsoft Authenticator from the App Store (iOS) or Google Play (Android)
   - Open the app and select "Add account"
   - Choose "Work or school account"

3. **Scan QR Code**
   - Log into the company portal (portal.company.com)
   - Navigate to Security Settings > MFA Setup
   - A QR code will be displayed
   - Scan the QR code with your Authenticator app

4. **Verify Setup**
   - The app will display a 6-digit code
   - Enter this code on the portal to verify
   - You may be asked to approve a test sign-in

5. **Complete Registration**
   - Follow any additional prompts to complete setup
   - Save backup codes in a secure location

## Troubleshooting

- **Can't scan QR code**: Ensure your camera has permission and lighting is adequate
- **App not receiving codes**: Check app notifications are enabled
- **Lost backup codes**: Contact IT support to regenerate

## Support

If you encounter issues, contact IT support or open a ticket.
